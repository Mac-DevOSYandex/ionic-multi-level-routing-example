The Ionic Conference is a week long conference if it really existed - just pretend - featuring talks from the iTeam (and not the A-Team as previously advertised). It is focused on Iconic applications being built with Ionic 4. This includes migrating apps from Ionic 1, Ionic 2 & Ionic 3, Angular concepts, Webpack, Sass and many other technologies used in Ionic 4, which, by the way, is really Ionic 2+. Tickets are completely sold out but, you can still get in at the door, it's more of a dog & poeny show and we’re expecting more than 1000 developers – making this the largest IonicConfest® hence ever!
